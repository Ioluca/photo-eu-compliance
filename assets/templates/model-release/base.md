The Model retains the right to:
- Request access to all images (GDPR Art. 15)
- Request deletion of images not yet published (GDPR Art. 17)
- Withdraw consent for future use (GDPR Art. 7(3))
- Object to specific uses (GDPR Art. 21)

Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing
based on consent before its withdrawal. Images already published or
licensed to third parties prior to withdrawal may not be recallable.
