**Subject: Reservation of rights under Directive (EU) 2019/790, Article 4(3) —
Opt-out from text and data mining for AI model training**

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing as the rights holder of photographic works to formally reserve
my rights pursuant to Article 4(3) of Directive (EU) 2019/790 of the
European Parliament and of the Council of 17 April 2019 on copyright and
related rights in the Digital Single Market.

**1. Identification**
I am a professional photographer operating in [Country/Countries].
My works are published on the following platforms and websites:
- [Website URL]
- [Stock platform profile URLs]
- [Social media profile URLs]

**2. Reservation of Rights**
I hereby expressly reserve all rights regarding the reproduction and
extraction of my photographic works for the purposes of text and data mining,
including but not limited to the training of artificial intelligence models
and machine learning systems.

This reservation covers:
- All photographic works I have authored to date
- All future photographic works I will create
- All derivative works and versions thereof

**3. Technical Measures Already Implemented**
I have implemented the following machine-readable opt-out measures:
- robots.txt directives blocking AI crawlers on my website(s)
- Meta tags (noai, noimageai) on pages containing my works
- IPTC metadata with explicit copyright notices on my image files

**4. Request**
I request that you:
a) Confirm receipt of this reservation of rights within 30 days
b) Remove my works from any training datasets used for your AI models
c) Implement measures to prevent future inclusion of my works
d) Confirm completion of the above actions

**5. Legal Basis**
This request is made pursuant to:
- Directive (EU) 2019/790, Article 4(3)
- Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (AI Act), Article 53(1)(d)
- The General-Purpose AI Code of Practice (July 2025), Copyright Chapter

I reserve the right to take further legal action if this request is not
addressed within the stated timeframe.

Yours faithfully,

[Signature]
[Full Name]
