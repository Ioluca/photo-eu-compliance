# Spain — National Photography Law Reference

## Key Legislation
- **LO 1/1982** — Protección del derecho al honor, intimidad y propia imagen
- **LOPDGDD (LO 3/2018)** — GDPR implementation
- **Real Decreto Legislativo 1/1996** — Ley de Propiedad Intelectual

---

## Right to Image (Derecho a la propia imagen)

### LO 1/1982, Art. 7-8

**Art. 7:** Unauthorized capture, reproduction, or publication of a person's
image in places of their private life is an intromission on their image rights.

**Art. 8 — Exceptions:**
1. Persons exercising a public office or profession of public notoriety,
   when the image is captured during a public act or in public places
2. Caricature, in accordance with social custom
3. Graphic information on a public event, even if incidentally portraying individuals

**Practical interpretation:** More permissive than France or Germany for street
photography during public events. Individual portraits still require consent.

## AI-Specific: Collecting Society Initiative
Spain has been the first EU country to explore **collective licensing for AI
training**. VEGAP (visual artists collecting society) has proposed mechanisms
for AI companies to license visual works collectively, rather than individual
opt-out. This is experimental and not yet in force.
